Services

Currently I offer two types of websites. I can build a new one from the ground up or redesign what you currently have.

Basic websites are the standard type that you will need a developer to update. They will go in and modify the code for you. These tend to cost less up front.

Wordpress websites are a website designed for you to have the ability to edit the content of your site. Things like adding testimonials or changing paragraphs become something you can do in your free time. There are many options for this sort of thing, I can create a custom theme, modify an existing one, or help you buy a theme you love and get it set up.

Don't have time to maintain a website? I can do that for you as well! We will plan out how often you need your site updated and go from there.
