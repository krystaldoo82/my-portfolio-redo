About

Krystal is a free-spirited wife and mother to three teenagers located in rural Kansas. She got her start playing with code during the time MySpace was popular. She was always changing themes and helping others do the same. After that she began helping others make Wordpress blogs with the available themes, and even built a site or two with the free platforms available. But she always wanted to learn more and be the one in control of how everything worked. It wasn't until she found an advertisement for a web development class that things began to click. Suddenly she found a passion and spent months learning what it took to build sites that not only looked good in the browser, but on mobile devices as well.

When not writing code Krystal enjoys reading, playing Xbox, cooking for her family, taking nature walks (Especially during asparagus and mushroom seasons!), and taking a drive with the windows down.
